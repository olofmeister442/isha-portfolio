Adulting With MindPeers

Brief: Namita Thapar was onboarded for a campaign around mental health. The challenge was to come up with something that’s not too niche but still has a campaign identity of its own.

We picked ‘adulting and life transitions’ as a theme and approached it from four perspectives:

  • A clinical study (worked well with PR)
  • Lived experiences (worked well with influencer onboard)
  • Humour oriented (worked well with digital landscape)
  • A pan India college roadshow (worked well for offline engagements)

Impact: The campaign ran for about 45 days. It helped MindPeers increase revenue by 38% and move up in terms of brand equity from #6 to #2.
